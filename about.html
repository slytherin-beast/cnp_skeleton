Who We Are

Cultivator is a globally recognized leader in certified organic herbal ingredients. We have spent over two decades empowering wellness brands with transparency, sustainability, and Ayurvedic heritage. Our commitment to purity and traceability defines every product we deliver.

Global Market Coverage

Our products are trusted in over 25 countries across five continents. We take pride in empowering wellness globally with partnerships built on sustainability and transparency.

Empowering Rural Communities

We uplift thousands of farmers through organic farming training, fair wages, and long-term partnerships. Our rural education programs focus on sustainable practices, women empowerment, and regenerative agriculture.
