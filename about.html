Who We Are

Cultivator is a globally recognized leader in certified organic herbal ingredients. We have spent over two decades empowering wellness brands with transparency, sustainability, and Ayurvedic heritage. Our commitment to purity and traceability defines every product we deliver.

Our Journey

2000 - Foundation

Cultivator was founded with a mission to offer authentic, organic herbs globally.

2005 - Certification

Achieved first USDA Organic and EU Organic certifications for Indian-grown herbs.

2010 - Expansion

We expanded our reach to more than 20 countries with increased organic certifications.

2015 - New Facilities

Built a state-of-the-art processing center and implemented cleanroom packaging.

2020 - Sustainability Shift

All operations turned carbon-neutral and solar-powered, setting a benchmark in eco-conscious trade.

2022 - New Partnerships

Collaborated with global wellness brands to develop Ayurvedic-based hair care.

2023 - Innovation Launch

Introduced AI-powered farming advisory for rural farmers in India and overseas partners.

2024 - Sustainability Awards

Recognized globally for our regenerative farming and social impact across supply chains.

2025 - Ayurvedic R&D

Launched an R&D division focused on Ayurvedic clinical testing and formulation.
